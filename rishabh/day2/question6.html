The National Institute of Engineering and Technology Greater Noida
(also known as NIET Gr Noida) is managed by the City Educational &
Social Welfare Society and is approved by the UGC. NIET is accredited
with an 'A' Grade by the NAAC and the NBA and was established in 2001.
Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology is ranked 42 by NIRF
2023 under the Pharmacy category

Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology
is a member of the Delnet Developing Library Network,
The Indian Society for Technical Education, the Computer
Society of India, the International Association of Engineers,
